## 2. Best-fit target roles

### Highest-priority role families

1. **Chief Operating Officer (COO)**
   - Best fit at Series C–E companies or established growth businesses with connected products, field operations, industrial technology, mobility, logistics, or hardware-enabled services.
   - Strongest where the company needs a hands-on executive to install operating cadence, commercial infrastructure, cross-functional accountability, financial discipline, and scalable delivery mechanisms.

2. **Chief Commercial Officer (CCO) / Commercial Executive**
   - Best fit where commercial leadership includes GTM design, partnerships, pricing, revenue operations, customer success, implementation, and market expansion—not only direct enterprise sales management.
   - Especially strong for companies commercializing technically complex products with long sales cycles, deployment dependencies, channel partners, or technical buyers.

3. **VP / Head of Commercial Operations**
   - Scope may include revenue strategy, GTM operations, pricing, commercial planning, partner strategy, sales enablement, customer lifecycle, and execution across Product and Sales.

4. **VP / Head of Strategy & Operations**
   - Best fit as an enterprise or business-unit role directly supporting a GM, CEO, President, or product-line executive.
   - Strongest where strategy must be translated into execution systems, operating metrics, investment decisions, and cross-functional programs.

5. **Executive Chief of Staff / Chief of Staff to CEO, President, or GM**
   - Strong fit where the role has real authority to drive strategic initiatives, operating cadence, cross-functional decision-making, and business transformation.
   - Avoid purely administrative Chief of Staff positions.

6. **General Manager / Business Unit Leader**
   - Strong fit for connected-product, IoT, mobility, industrial, or infrastructure businesses requiring commercial ownership, operating accountability, strategic partnerships, and P&L discipline.

### Secondary role families

- VP Business Operations
- VP Revenue Strategy and Operations
- VP Commercial Strategy
- Director / Senior Director, EV Strategy & Operations
- Director / Senior Director, Connected Products Strategy & Operations
- Director / Senior Director, Market Development or Strategic Partnerships
- Head of Commercial, Regional GM, or Market Operations Leader for autonomous logistics, mobility, or field-deployed technology

### Titles to deprioritize unless the scope is materially broader

- Director or VP of Customer Success
- Sales Operations leader
- Program Manager
- Product Manager / Product Owner
- Generic SaaS operations roles without physical-product, technical-deployment, or commercial-strategy exposure
